kesari Recipe

Introduction

Today am going to teach how to make pineapple kesari.Pineappe kesari color would be yellow in color and it looks like a halwa and taste will be awesome.Lets start to make a kesari

First heat the pan and add one teaspoon of ghee and add one cup of sooji/rava make it roast,then take the roasted rava from the pan and keep it away.Then roast the cashewnuts and some raisins once its getting roasted take them away too.Then add three cups of water for one cup of rava.add apinch of salt for enhancing the sweet.Once water getting started to boiling then dd rava little by little without getting lumps.Once rava started observing water then add sugar then add pineapple chunks once all get dried add apinch of saffron,roasted cashes and raisins,then add some ghee as much us you need.Garnishing with more pineapple chuns.Now ready for plating.Nice and yummy pineappe kesari is ready to eat.

Ingredients

S.No Description Measurement
1 Rava 1 Cup
2 Water 3 Cups
3 Sugar 2 Cups
4 Pineapple 1 Cup
5 Ghee,cashews,raisins,saffron 2 Tablespoon
